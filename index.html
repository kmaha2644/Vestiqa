About

High-quality, trendy, and affordable fashion for you.

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    The years 2009 to 2011 marked the true beginning of our brand. What started as a simple vision slowly evolved into a promising journey. Through hard work, learning, and commitment to quality, we laid the groundwork that continues to guide our growth.

  • March 2011

    An Agency is Born

    In March 2011, our vision took a major step forward as we officially formed our agency. What began as a small idea transformed into a dedicated team committed to delivering quality, creativity, and meaningful results.

  • December 2015

    Transition to Full Service

    In December 2015, we expanded our capabilities and officially transitioned into a full-service agency. This step allowed us to offer a complete range of solutions under one roof, strengthening our brand and improving the experience for every client.

  • July 2020

    Phase Two Expansion

    In July 2020, Phase Two Expansion officially began, allowing us to scale our operations, introduce new features, and elevate the overall quality of our work.
